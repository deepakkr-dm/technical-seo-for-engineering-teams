# URL Architecture Patterns (Engineering SEO)

URLs should reflect capability clusters, not marketing campaigns.

---

## Principles

- Keep URLs stable (avoid dates unless required)
- Use capability-based grouping
- Avoid duplicate intent across pages

---

## Example Capability Clusters

- /ai-consulting-for-healthcare
- /ai-consulting-for-fintech
- /mobile-performance-engineering
- /backend-development
- /frontend-development

---

## Mapping Rule

One URL = one primary intent.

Bad:
- /ai-development-services
- /ai-development-company
- /ai-app-development

Good:
- /ai-development
  - /ai-development/llm-apps
  - /ai-development/ai-agents
  - /ai-development/rag-systems
