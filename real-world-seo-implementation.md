# Real-World SEO Implementation

## Project Overview
- Industry: AI/Software Development
- Objective: Optimize technical SEO for an AI-native product website.

## Problem
The client’s product website was not ranking well despite good content. It was due to poor technical SEO, lack of clear URL structure, and missing schema markup.

## Solution
We implemented the **Technical SEO for Engineering Teams** framework:
- Optimized URL structure to reflect product architecture
- Added relevant schema (Product, Article, FAQPage) to boost visibility
- Improved internal linking and content structure

## Results
- Increased organic traffic by 30% in 6 months
- Improved ranking for technical terms and AI product keywords
- Boosted developer trust through clear, structured documentation
